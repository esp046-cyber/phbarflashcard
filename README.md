# phbarflashcard
comprehensive 2026 Philippine Bar Exam flashcard reviewer app with:
Features:
8 Bar Subjects: Civil Law, Criminal Law, Remedial Law, Political Law, Taxation, Labor Law, Mercantile Law, and Legal Ethics
10 flashcards per subject (80 total cards) covering key doctrines, definitions, and concepts
Mobile-optimized with 1% margins and small font titles as requested
Interactive flip cards - tap to reveal answers
Swipe navigation - swipe left/right to navigate cards
Keyboard support - arrow keys to navigate, spacebar to flip
Shuffle function - randomize cards for better retention
Progress tracking - visual progress bar and statistics
Professional bar exam theme - dark blue/gold color scheme reminiscent of legal tradition
The app works entirely offline once loaded and tracks your study progress. Each subject contains relevant bar exam topics that are commonly tested. The design is clean, focused, and optimized for mobile study sessions.
